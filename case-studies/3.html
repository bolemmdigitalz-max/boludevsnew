Project Overview

Building a Digital Front Door for a Growing Business

The client was a Lagos-based service company operating without a website. Their only digital presence was a WhatsApp number shared on physical flyers — leading to missed enquiries from customers searching online.

My goal was to build a professional, fast-loading website that immediately communicated trust, showcased their services clearly, and converted visitors into paying customers through a simple WhatsApp-first CTA flow.

Client Goals

  • Look professional and credible online
  • Rank in Google for local service searches
  • Capture customer enquiries via WhatsApp
  • Mobile-first for Lagos Android users
The Challenge

What Was Holding the Business Back

Without a website, the business was invisible to customers searching on Google. Competitors with even basic web presences were capturing the majority of digital enquiries. The client also had no way to showcase their work, build credibility, or follow up with prospects professionally.

  • No online presence or Google discoverability
  • No portfolio or social proof to show prospects
  • Relying entirely on word-of-mouth referrals
  • Losing customers to competitors with websites
The Solution

What I Built and Why

I designed and built a clean, fast, fully responsive website optimised for mobile-first Nigerian users. The site features a compelling hero section, a clear services breakdown, social proof via testimonials, and a frictionless WhatsApp CTA flow that converts visitors in seconds.

The site was deployed to Vercel with a custom .com.ng domain from WhoGoHost, auto-configured SSL, and SEO meta tags targeting the client's local search keywords. Delivered in under 72 hours.
